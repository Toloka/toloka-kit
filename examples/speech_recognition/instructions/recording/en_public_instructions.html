Each task contains words and phrases. You need to read and record them. Make a new recording for each phrase, even if it has already been used in other tasks. iOS (iPhone, iPad) Toloka App
  1. Tap . The voice recorder will appear at the bottom of the screen, and recording will start.
  2. Say the word or phrase from the task. Start talking only after the mic turns on so that the beginning of the phrase is not cut off.
  3. When you’re finished, tap .
  4. Save the recording if everything is OK. If you make a mistake, tap Start over.
  5. When the file is uploaded, its number will appear in the task. Now you can move on to the next task.

Browser (Toloka Web-Version)
  1. Click . The file explorer will appear on the screen.
  2. Choose recorded audio file.
  3. When the file is chosen, its name will appear in the task. If recording name is blinking, it means that the uploading has not completed yet. Wait until it end. 
  4. Upload the recording if everything is OK. If you make a mistake, click cross and try again. Now you can move on to the next task.

Checking completed tasks

We usually check tasks within a few days. The maximum time for checking a task is 21 days.

Possible reasons for rejecting the task:

  • Recordings do not contain the words and phrases from the task.
  • Words and phrases are not recorded fully or some of the words are cut off.
  • Recordings are too quiet or indistinguishable because of background noises.
  • The same recording is attached to several tasks.
