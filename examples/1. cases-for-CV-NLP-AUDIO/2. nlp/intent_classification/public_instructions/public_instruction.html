Please choose the right category and class of intent for a given text.
If the query is within the kitchen category, the possible intents are:
  • Restaurant review - anything related to whether a restaurant is worth the visit
  • Restaurant reservation - intention to reserve a table in the restaurant
  • Nutritional information - intention to know information about how healthy food or amount of calories
  • Recipe - anything related to how to prepare food or food substitution
If the query is within the travel category, the possible intents are:
  • Book a hotel - anything related to reservation a flight or hotel
  • Timezone - intention to get information about timezone
  • Travel suggestion - query related to recommendation on a travel
  • Exchange rate - intention to know price to buy such currency
  • Vaccine - query which contains information about vaccines in certain region

Text:
are bananas rich in calories

Kitchen
